Consign With SHENBERG

We make consigning simple and reliable — from a quick evaluation through to logistics, cataloguing and sale. Below is a short walkthrough of how the process works and what you can expect.

How It Works

  1. Step 1 — Submit Your Request

    Send us a message with details about the items you wish to sell. Attach photos, if available, for a quick evaluation.

  2. Step 2 — Contract Agreement

    Once your items are approved, we will send you a sale contract. Fill out the contract and return it to confirm your participation.

  3. Step 3 — Logistics Arranged by Us

    We can organize the pickup of your items from almost any country. Simply provide the address, and we'll arrange a courier to collect your lots at your convenience.

  4. Step 4 — Auction Listing

    After receiving your items, our team will handle all the preparation, including cataloging and professional presentation for the auction.
